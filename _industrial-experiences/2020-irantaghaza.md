## Key Achievements

- Conceptualized and developed Iran's first demand-oriented marketplace platform as an inverse model to Divar
- Built complete full-stack web application from scratch using modern web technologies
- Designed and implemented user-friendly interface for posting and browsing demand requests
- Developed matching algorithm to connect demand posts with potential suppliers
- Created real-time notification system for demand-supply matching
- Established the foundational business model and operational framework

## Outcome

- Platform successfully launched with initial user acquisition
- Demonstrated market need for demand-side marketplace concept
- Startup dissolved after one month of operation due to financial constraints and funding challenges
- Gained valuable experience in startup development, product-market fit validation, and the challenges of early-stage venture sustainability
