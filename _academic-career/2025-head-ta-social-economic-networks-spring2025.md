- Conducted regular problem-solving sessions for each assignment
- Organized comprehensive review sessions for midterm and final exam preparation
- Provided specialized guidance in social and economic network analysis methodologies

- Designed and implemented research-oriented project frameworks
- Evaluated projects using innovative assessment criteria focused on novel ideas
- Ensured transparent and equitable evaluation processes for all student projects
- Maintained team diversity in assignment groups while optimizing team sizes

### Key Achievements

As my inaugural experience as Head TA, I successfully navigated the challenges of limited pre-existing course materials by developing comprehensive content from scratch. I established continuous feedback mechanisms and ensured timely release of assignments with detailed solutions. Student satisfaction surveys reflected exceptionally positive feedback regarding course delivery and support systems.

### Personal Reflection

This role provided an incredibly rewarding professional experience. I simultaneously contributed to assignment design, midterm and final exam preparation, and project management teams while overseeing all these components. Additionally, I served as an exam proctor during assessment periods. By strategically diversifying team compositions and maintaining optimal team sizes, I facilitated rapid and high-quality assignment design and evaluation. I am profoundly grateful for this leadership opportunity and cherish the invaluable experience gained through this role. The comprehensive involvement across all aspects of course delivery—from exam supervision to project management—has been both challenging and immensely fulfilling.
