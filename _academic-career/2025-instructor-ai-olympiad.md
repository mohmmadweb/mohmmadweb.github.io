## Course Details

**Course:** Artificial Intelligence Olympiad - Phase 2
**Program:** Young Scholars Club AI Olympiad Training
**Supervisor:** [Dr. Soleymani](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=S1U0KlgAAAAJ&hl=en)

**Teaching Topics:**

- Advanced Data Augmentation techniques for machine learning
- Early Stopping methods and optimization strategies
- Model regularization and prevention of overfitting
- Performance optimization in AI systems

**Responsibilities:**

- Preparing and delivering lectures on specialized AI topics
- Designing practical exercises and projects for talented students
- Mentoring students for national AI Olympiad competitions
- Evaluating student progress and providing feedback
- Collaborating with other instructors in curriculum development

**About AI Olympiad:**
The Artificial Intelligence Olympiad is a national competition that identifies and nurtures talented students in AI and machine learning. Phase 2 focuses on advanced concepts and practical implementations, preparing students for complex AI challenges and research-oriented projects.
